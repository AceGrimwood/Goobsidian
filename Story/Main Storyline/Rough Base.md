# Arc 1: Nanites nanites…

### Intro / Tutorial

- The player plays through a memory of combat—revealed to be a mental toughness simulation.
- Transitions into a practical tutorial simulating dispatch duties and introducing standard/illegal Nanite enemies.

### Mission 1: The Local Gang

- Basic test mission with no Nanites involved initially: the player fights a gang vandalizing a local area.
- **Boss fight:** Introduces the rage mode mechanic
- **Narrative hook:** Searching the boss’s phone reveals they were scheduled to buy Nanites from a darknet dealer. Since it was anonymous, the player goes undercover to the meeting.

### Mission 2: The Dealer & the Unstable Tech

- The player meets the dealer, who shows off the new _Unstable Nanites_ and their 1-minute replication cycle.
- **Tower vs. tower combat:** The dealer uses their own turrets to demonstrate on enemies. The player is invited to deploy towers too. The dealer recognizes the player’s tech, blows their cover, and programs turrets to attack the player’s towers.
- **Boss fight:** The dealer enters the field, throwing grenades and using abilities. (When low On health, becomes faster)
- **Climax (using backend mechanics):** Once defeated, the dealer triggers a _Dynamic Faction Switch_ (becoming an ally so towers stop shooting). They surrender and try to spill secrets—until the supplier’s hidden ultra-unstable (seconds-to-replicate) Nanites in their arm violently corrupt and explode them, kicking off the deeper mystery of the overarching plot.
- We grab a small sample (proximity prompt or item drop from gamemode) of the nanites to be researched in a lab.
