### **Starter & Early-Game Towers**

- **Ranger**
    - Free starter tower. Fires in bursts with an M16A2. Top path gains stealth detection and converts to full-auto; bottom path gets grenades and incendiary fire pools for crowd control.
- **Shotgunner**
    - Costs 450 Gold. Fires a spread of bullets in an arc. Top path gains stealth detection, faster fire rate, and a healing ability; bottom path focuses on piercing through multiple enemies and ignoring bullet resistance, with an unstun ability at max level.
- **Operator**
    - Costs 1,250 Gold. Unlocked at Rank 15. Spawns a Humvee at max bottom path. Solid mid-game tower with good health and damage output.
- **Mine Layer**
    - Costs 1,450 Gold. Places mines on the path that explode when enemies walk over them. Top path focuses on faster mine placement, larger splash, and stealth detection; bottom path focuses on high-damage thermite mines with very long lifetime and explosive resistance piercing.
- **Cryo Blaster**
    - Costs 2,000 Gold. Freezes enemies but deals no damage until later upgrades. Top path freezes hordes and ignores ice resistance; bottom path freezes single strong enemies/bosses and gains stealth detection.
- **Missile Trooper**
    - Costs 2,000 Gold. Explosive splash damage with a SMAW. Top path gets massive range, stealth detection, and a 3x primary target multiplier for bosses; bottom path gets huge splash radius and rapid fire for crowds.
- **Patrol Boat**
    - Costs 2,150 Gold. Water-only tower. Top path switches to explosive damage with massive range and passive missile strikes; bottom path switches to fire damage with a flamethrower, high burn DPS, and stun immunity.
- **Farm**
    - Costs 2,200 Gold. Generates cash per wave. Top path increases income and crate generation speed; bottom path provides tower discounts and sellback bonuses.
- **Barracks**
    - Costs 2,350 Gold. Spawns Scout units that move along the path and attack enemies. Top path upgrades to Rail Troopers with laser damage and stealth detection; bottom path spawns M202 Troopers, Rioters, and Flamethrowers for crowd control.
- **Grenadier**
    - Costs 2,750 Gold. Throws grenades for splash damage. Top path gets cheap stealth detection and higher DPS; bottom path stuns enemies (up to 5 at max level) and ignores explosive resistance.

### **Mid-Game Towers**

- **EDJ**
    - Costs 3,200 Gold. Support tower (no damage). Top path buffs nearby tower firerate by up to 60% and range by 30%; bottom path discounts upgrades by up to 30% and increases sellback value. Essential for Expert/Endless modes.
- **John**
    - Costs 4,500 Gold. Tribute to the game's creator. Top path ignores bullet resistance early, then becomes a support tower with a 50% damage buff ability and stealth detection; bottom path uses a shotgun spread and axe attack for crowd control, with a passive railgun.
- **Toxicnator**
    - Costs 6,500 Gold. Deals toxic damage over time and reduces enemy resistances by up to 30% (15% on bosses). Has an ability that makes enemies take more damage from all sources.
- **Sentry**
    - Costs 7,250 Gold. Cheap placement cost with a limited cone of view. Top path gains stealth detection and ignores bullet resistance; bottom path gets a minigun, stun immunity, and passive rocket attacks.
- **Scout Helicopter**
    - Newer aerial tower. Limited data; appears to be a lighter aerial unit focused on mobility and early air defense.
- **AA Turret**
    - Anti-air specialized tower. Limited detailed data in sources.
- **Juggernaut**
    - Costs 13,250 Gold. Heavy minigun tower. Top path gets explosive HE Rounds ability and ignores bullet resistance; bottom path gets stealth detection, stun immunity, and Overcharge ability for massive single-target DPS.
- **Railgunner**
    - Costs 15,000 Gold. Extremely high single-target damage with long reload. Top path gets faster reload, stealth detection, and pierce; bottom path gets massive damage (up to 8,010 per shot), stun immunity, and huge range but requires aiming time.
- **Machine Gunner**
    - Unlocked at Rank 45, costs 18,000 Gold. Restricted to a 110° firing arc. Top path gets 220° motion, pierce up to 4 enemies, air targeting, and stun immunity; bottom path gets laser damage, stealth detection, and an Overcharge ability that temporarily massively boosts DPS and range.

### **Support & Healing**

- **Medic**
    - Healing support tower. Top path focuses on massive healing per second and Uber ability (invulnerability for 12-25 seconds); bottom path focuses on damage buffs via Kritz ability (100-200% damage boost for 10-14 seconds).
- **Commander**
    - Single-path support tower. Buffs nearby tower damage by up to 7% and range by up to 30%. Abilities include Call to Arms (damage buff + shooting), Stryker Call (spawns APC), and Airstrike (2,000 explosive damage in an area).

### **Event & Limited Towers**

- **Cryo Ranger**
    - 2023 XMAS Event. Modified Ranger that fires cryogenic rounds. Top path gets cheap stealth detection and freezing; bottom path gets cryo grenades and passive freeze fields.
- **Z.E.D.**
    - Tower Battles collab event. Spawns Zombie Extermination Device units with autocannons and rockets. Level 5 unit has 10,000 HP and nearly 3,000 DPS. Single path only.
- **Scarecrow**
    - Event bait tower. Extremely cheap placement cost, no attacks, meant purely to absorb enemy attacks.
- **Flame Trooper**
    - The Awakened Invasion event. Flamethrower tower that applies burn damage and debuffs enemies to take +5% more damage. Fire Ring ability at max level. No stealth detection.
- **Relic**
    - The Awakened Invasion event. Spawns medieval units (Bandits, Zealots, Archers, Wardens, Seraphs, and Overseer Prime). Max level costs $1,000,000 and summons the Overseer Prime (150,000 HP, 21,000+ DPS cleaving claw).
- **Cryo Helicopter**
    - 2024 XMAS Event. Aerial support that freezes up to 3 enemies at once. Can move anywhere on the map but cannot receive buffs. Gains stealth detection, boss freezing, and cryo missiles.
- **Ice Breaker**
    - Christmas II Nightmare event. Melee tower that freezes enemies with each attack. Has Ice Shard and Snow Storm abilities for crowd control.
- **Troll Tower**
    - 2025 April Fools event. Bait tower based on the Troll Face meme. Random ability gives either a 5% damage buff, heals towers by 80%, or unstuns nearby towers.
- **Goo Gunner**
    - Slime Event. Deals toxic damage. Limited data but appears to function similarly to Toxicnator with slime-themed mechanics.
- **Shock Trooper**
    - Event tower. Uses chain lightning attacks. First tower with increased damage mechanic based on chaining.
- **Cryo Mortar**
    - 2025 XMAS Event. Long-range mortar that freezes and slows enemies in an area. Has minimum range, making it best placed far from the path. Can freeze bosses and ignore ice resistance at max level.
- **Psycho Slayer**
    - Event tower. Described as a "jack of all trades" with multiple damage types to counter various resistances.
- **Chicken House**
    - Event tower. Limited data available.
- **Bubble Blower**
    - Event tower. Limited data available.

### **Late-Game & Ultimate Towers**

- **Warship**
    - Unlocked at Rank 100, costs 75,000 Gold. Water-only. Top path evolves into a massive splash-damage battleship with 17 independently targeting attacks and map-wide range; bottom path focuses on single-target devastation (15,555 damage per shot) and a global stealth reveal ability. Costs over $300,000 to max out.
- **Armored Factory**
    - Spawns armored vehicles that move along the path while firing. Top path spawns Humvees and M2 Bradleys (ignore explosive resistance); bottom path spawns MRAPs and M1X Abrams tanks with massive health and DPS.
- **Golden Juggernaut**
    - Golden tower variant. Much higher damage, range, and health than regular Juggernaut. Has Reboot Systems ability to unstun itself and gain a damage buff. One of the most expensive towers in the game.

### **Notable Mechanics Across Towers**

- **Dual Path System**: Most towers have a Top Path and Bottom Path that specialize differently (e.g., single-target vs. crowd control, or damage vs. support).
- **Health System**: Towers have health and can be killed by enemy/boss attacks. Some can rebuild (Sentry, XWM Turret) or be healed (Medic).
- **Damage Types**: Bullet, Fire, Explosive, Laser, Ice, Toxic, Melee, Golden, Collision, Lightning. Enemies have resistances to these.
- **Stealth Detection**: Required to target invisible enemies. Provided by upgrades on many towers or permanently by some (Laser Gunner base level).
- **Resistance Piercing**: Many towers can ignore specific resistances (e.g., John ignores bullet resistance; Mine Layer ignores explosive resistance).
