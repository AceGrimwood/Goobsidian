### **Basic Grunts**

- **Zombie / Fast Zombie / Heavy Zombie** The basic trio. Slow, fast, and tanky variants. No special mechanics.
- **Armored Zombie** First enemy with Bullet resistance, teaching players to mix damage types.
- **Chained** Slightly faster heavy zombie. No unique abilities.
- **Armored Heavy Zombie** Combines heavy HP with bullet resistance.
- **Mutated Zombie** Spawns Mutated Offspring upon death, creating swarm pressure.
- **Hazmat Zombie** Fire resistant. Introduces elemental resistance mechanics.
- **Radioactive Zombie** Explodes on death, damaging nearby towers.
- **Nuclear Zombie** Upgraded radioactive variant with higher damage death explosion.

### **Intermediate Threats**

- **Abomination** First major HP gatecheck. High health, slightly fast speed. No abilities but tests raw DPS.
- **Plague Doctor** Mini-boss. Spawns Resurrected Rangers (Slow, Armored, and normal variants) upon death, creating a second wave of threats.
- **Grenadier (Enemy)** Throws grenades at towers, dealing splash damage and stunning.
- **Elite Grenadier** Upgraded version that spawns Shockers upon death.
- **Shocker** Explodes on death, emitting a shockwave that stuns all nearby towers for 5 seconds.
- **Monster** High HP, slow speed. Basic tank.
- **Infiltrator** Extremely fast (8.0 speed), 85% Explosive resistance. Forces non-explosive counters.
- **Charger Boss** High HP, no resistances. Basic mini-boss.
- **Smasher** Stuns towers with its hammer. Revived Smasher variant appears in later modes.
- **Predator** Fast, high HP. Spawns Hunters and Snow Predators. First enemy with summon mechanics.
- **Revived Abomination** Stronger version with more HP, appears in multiple modes.

### **Resurrected / Summoned**

- **Resurrected Slow Ranger** Slow but tanky, spawned by Plague Doctor.
- **Resurrected Ranger / Armored Ranger** Standard and bullet-resistant variants.
- **Resurrected Juggernaut / Juggernaut 2** Extremely tanky with high bullet and explosive resistance.
- **Resurrected John** Spawns in Endless Mode. Uses John's abilities against players.
- **Resurrected Laser Gunner** High laser resistance, spawned by late-wave bosses.
- **Knight** Melee-resistant, spawned by Plague Doctor.

### **Multipliers (Cloning)**

- **Multiplier 1** On death, clones a nearby enemy with 1.3x speed buff and +30% DPS. Clones give no cash. Wave 69 Endless version has 84,000x HP as a dev joke.
- **Multiplier 2** Same mechanic but appears in hordes.
- **Multiplier 3** Endless/Nightmare variant. Clones 3 enemies.
- **Multiplier X** Endless Wave 92. Can clone BOSSES. Fully heals multiplied enemies, gives 15% speed buff. No cash from clones.
- **Multiplier T** Tower Battles Nightmare / Endless Wave 177 variant.

### **Expert Mode**

- **Jumper** Uses **Leap** to jump 23 studs forward while invulnerable, bypassing frontlines. One of the most hated enemies.
- **Super Heavy Titan** Massive HP pool for its early wave appearance. Bullet resistant.
- **Speed Deployer** Spawns multiple fast enemies upon death.
- **Armored Runner** 75% Bullet resistance, 50% Ice resistance. Forces fire/explosive/laser damage.
- **Pilot** Aerial enemy. Requires anti-air or high-range towers.
- **Titan Charger** Fast, 16,750 HP + 2,500 shield. 85% Laser, 80% Golden, 90% Collision resistance. Counters laser/golden/spawner strategies.
- **VX-Drone** Aerial, high speed. Missile attacks stun towers.
- **X Juggernaut** Massive HP, stun immune, 75% bullet resistance.
- **X Ordinal** Support enemy that buffs nearby enemies.
- **Damage Deployer** Drops deployables that damage towers over time.
- **Zombie Drop Pod** Drops from sky, spawns zombies on impact.

### **Expert Mode Bosses (The Big Three)**

- **Phoebus** Wave 30 boss. **Shield spam** makes him invincible during charges. Explosion bypasses shields. Most annoying of the trio.
- **Zeus** Wave 30 boss. High HP, ranged lightning attacks. Less threatening than Phoebus but can wipe defenses if ignored.
- **Artemis** Wave 30 boss. Ranged attacks, high DPS. Can kill expensive towers quickly if not countered with AA.

### **Eradicator Line**

- **Eradicator** Wave 32 boss. First true boss. High HP, missile barrages, minigun sweep.
- **Eradicator MK II** Wave 40/42. **First enemy to stealth itself** (Visual Interference System). Heals 42,000 HP and goes invisible for 10s. **Mechanical Terror** resets all cooldowns at low HP.
- **Eradicator MK II+** Upgraded version. **Explosive Exuviation** at 45% HP: 90% damage reduction, blasts armor off, stuns all towers, then goes stealth and heals 175,000 HP. Spawns Missile/Healing drones.
- **Eradicator MK IV** Wave 50. Even stronger variant with more resistances.
- **Eradicator MK I Combat** Wave 60. Combat-focused variant with different attack patterns.
- **Eradidog** Dog variant, spawned by other Eradicators. Fast, annoying.

### **Predator Line**

- **Hunter** Fast, spawned by Predator/Apex.
- **Snow Predator** Ice-themed variant, appears in XMAS events.
- **Apex Predator** Endless Wave 70. Ultimate Predator. **Apex Call** summons 7 Predators + 7 Snow Predators + 3 Hunters. **Missile Burst** fires 4 missiles at farthest towers (up to 23,000 damage). **Seismic Slam** stuns in 18-stud radius. **Triple Pulse** fires 3 lasers at most expensive tower (up to 25,000 damage). **Eyes Locked** beam deals 39,852 damage. **Core Collapse** on death: 100,000 damage explosion in 10-stud radius.
- **Alpha Predator** Nightmare/Ultra Nightmare variant. Nukes expensive towers with missiles.

### **Endless Late-Game**

- **Breacher** Wave 36. 6,500 HP + 3,000 shield. Very fast (7.0). 50% Laser/Golden resistance. Breaks energy-based defenses.
- **Ravage / Grumble / Herculean** Wave 42-47. High HP tanks that test sustained DPS.
- **Maniac** Wave 50. Fast, high damage. Spawns randomly.
- **Anti Golem** Wave 51. Counters golem/spawner strategies.
- **Suppressor** Wave 76. Exists to waste your time. No real threat but annoying.
- **Calamity** Wave 80. **The most hated enemy in TDX.** Massive HP sponge, walks extremely slow. Randomly **instakills towers** with cheap shots. You're more likely to die to its spawns than the boss itself. Forces players past Wave 80 to have perfect defense.
- **Goliath** Wave 83. 500,000 HP, 50% freeze resistance. Pure money bag if you have the DPS.
- **Multiplier X** Wave 92. 500,000 HP + 500,000 shield. Extremely fast. 100% ice immune. Can multiply bosses.
- **Assailant** Wave 130. 475,000 HP + 123,500 shield. 99% Laser, 99% Explosive, 99% Golden resistance. Counters Warships and Golden Juggernauts. Requires helicopters and top-path Juggernauts.
- **Jammer** Wave 135. Disables tower abilities in range.
- **Tank Toilet / John Toilet / SUPER REVIVED TOILET JOHN MK I9X** Toilet event references. High HP, meme-tier names, actual threats.
- **Void Apex Predator** Wave 200. Final boss before Endbringer.
- **Endbringer** Wave 201. **Run-ending boss.** **Into Oblivion** throws hammer at most expensive tower, **instakilling it**. **Callous Crushing** deals 1,000 AoE damage. Appears in Tower Battles Nightmare Wave 40 and Endless Wave 201.

### **Nightmare / Ultra Nightmare**

- **J-X** Fast, 90% bullet resistance, high HP. Knife attack stuns. Most common cause of Nightmare deaths.
- **Jager** **Defense destroyer.** If not killed ASAP, wipes all expensive towers. Golden Juggernauts, Refractors, everything dies. Only XWM Turrets survive due to rebuild.
- **Spawn Deployer / Ultra Spawner** Constantly spawns enemies. If ignored, overwhelms defense.
- **Dispenser** Gives shields to nearby enemies 24/7. Infuriating support unit.
- **Smasher-9X** Wave 39 Ultra Nightmare. 150,000 HP + 400,000 shield. **Palpitation** debuffs massive area. Stun immune.
- **Emperor** Wave 50. Spawns Clone MK I/II, Attack Drones, Healing Drones. Immune to War Machine punch throwback.
- **E-02** Wave 50. Companion to Emperor.
- **Striker** Halloween 2024 boss. Appears 5 times across nights. High damage, stuns towers, ludicrous HP. Most annoying dreadnought.
- **Battering Ram** Charges forward. If it leaks, run is over. Induces terror.
- **Chariot** Spawns Chariot Turrets on the path. Turrets spawncamp your units.
- **Nuclear Tank** Explodes on death, dealing massive damage. Counters Golden Juggernauts hard.
- **Iron Bastion** Attacks towers every millisecond. Non-stop aggression.
- **Purgatory** Targets aerial units and combat drones specifically.

### **Key Mechanics**

- **Stealth** All enemies can be stealthed, including bosses. Eradicator MK II was the first self-stealthing enemy.
- **Resistances** Bullet, Fire, Explosive, Laser, Ice, Melee, Golden, Collision. Forces mixed loadouts.
- **Death Effects** Explosions (Radioactive/Nuclear Zombie), stuns (Shocker), spawns (Mutated Zombie, Plague Doctor, Multipliers).
- **Invulnerability Phases** Phoebus shield, Jumper Leap, Eradicator MK II+ Explosive Exuviation.
- **Instakill** Endbringer's hammer, Calamity's random kills.
- **Spawner Counters** Titan Charger (90% collision resist), Jager (wipes expensive towers).
- **Support/Healing** Healing Drones, Dispenser shields, Elite Grenadier spawning Shockers.
